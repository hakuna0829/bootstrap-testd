How TESTD™ works

Private and secure COVID-19 test data

Our blockchain technology secures encrypted user-data
and limits access to it.
Your test data is captured and
stored on IBM’s Hyperledger,
which keeps any personal
information private but allows
your virus status to be
viewed by others when
distance can’t be maintained.

How to take a test

TESTD allows employees a safe return to work –
no risk of exposure when accurate,
current tests can be displayed

Download the TESTD app
and create an account.
A test kit and test-taker are matched when you scan
the QR code on the test kit. That locks it to the
biometric ID inputs on the user’s phone.
The test-taking is witnessed by a verified
employee – either in person or by
a telemedicine technician, depending on the
process your employer has chosen.
The verified test result is recorded confidentially
and made accessible via TESTD blockchain
technology on your smartphone app.
