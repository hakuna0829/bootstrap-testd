Our story

We are mission-driven

Like millions in our country and around the world, every member of our team has experience with the
COVID-19 virus, personally or through family members and friends. Because our work is focused on bringing
blockchain tools to small businesses, we chose to honor those lost to us by applying our technology to the
needs businesses face to get safely back to work. Blockchain technology gives employers the transparency they
need to prove the workforce has tested safe; gives your team the reporting tools they need to manage the
work staff and maintain privacy and gives customers the assurance they wonâ€™t be exposed.
