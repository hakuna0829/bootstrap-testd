TESTD™ use cases

Delivery Drivers

Whether it’s a hot cheeseburger from your favorite restaurant, fresh veggies from the local market, or a new pair of shoes from your favorite brand, America’s delivery drivers keep our economy moving. Ensuring they are free of COVID-19 gives your customers an outstanding customer experience and heps stop the spread of the disease.

Nurses

Nurses serve on the frontlines of the American healthcare system. As providers of in-home care and at clinics and hospitals, they are also on the frontline of the COVID-19 pandemic. Many hospitals are already providing regular testing for their staff, and TESTD is the perfect solution to help protect the safety of nurses, their families, and their patients.

Restaurants

The restaurant industry is one of the largest employers in the country, and dining out is usually a key part of any night out on the town. Getting our nation safely back to work means getting our restaurants back to full capacity. With TESTD, you can have confidence that your chefs, servers, and customers are all protected.

Travel

From airlines and cruise lines to hotels and vacation rental services, the COVID-19 pandemic has been devastating. Until travelers feel safe to take vacations and business trips, planes will remain grounded and cruise ships will stay anchored. With TESTD, you can give your employees and customers the confidence they need to get out and safely see the world.

Sporting Events

As a sports leagues get ready to re-start without fans in the stadium, we’ll miss the roar of the crowds if its TV only viewing. Fans already line up to pass through metal detectors at arenas, and with one extra safety check of a TESTD ID, we’ll soon be filling our stadiums to cheer on our favorite teams.

Education

Teachers and educators represent the single largest group of employees in America. While online classes have been able to keep some students engaged, nothing can replace the in-person classroom experience. Many college campuses face financial ruin as students choose to stay home and wait it out or do online school, rather than pay for room and board. With TESTD, you can safely and quickly get your campuses re-opened while protecting your students, teachers, and staff.
